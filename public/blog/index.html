Learning About ZK SNARKs

ZK SNARKs are one of the most amazing products of modern cutting edge cryptography. They let a prover secretly run a program of arbitrary complexity and generate a compact proof that any can verify that correctly ran the program. The program can have secret inputs and the prover reveals nothing to the user. Zk SNARKs have been deployed in the ZCash cryptocurrency and many other protocols are examining using them. Read more
